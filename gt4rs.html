An uncompromising driver's car designed to thrill, crafted for the absolute maximum.

The 718 Cayman GT4 RS takes everything that makes the mid-engine platform legendary and dials it up to eleven. At its heart lies the naturally aspirated 4.0-liter flat-six engine borrowed directly from the 911 GT3, delivering a spine-tingling crescendo that revs all the way to 9,000 rpm.

Aerodynamics and lightweight construction dictate every curve and intake on this car. From the striking swan-neck rear wing to the exposed carbon-fiber elements, this is a track weapon engineered for maximum downforce, razor-sharp agility, and unparalleled feedback.

Pure Adrenaline

Ultimately, the GT4 RS isn't just about shaving seconds off lap times; it's about the raw, unfiltered emotion of driving. With the engine breathing right behind your ears through the distinctive process air intakes, every shift and every corner becomes a visceral experience you will never forget.
