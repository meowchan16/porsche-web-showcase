The 911 Targa 4S is a tribute to a legend, blending iconic design with modern performance.

Defined by its characteristic silver roll bar and wraparound rear window, the Targa 4S offers a driving experience like no other. At the push of a button, the roof panel disappears behind the rear seats in a mechanical ballet that never fails to captivate.

Underneath its elegant silhouette lies the heart of a pure athlete. The twin-turbo flat-six engine provides effortless power, while the standard all-wheel-drive system ensures that the Targa remains poised and confident through every curve, regardless of the weather.

The Spirit of Freedom

The Targa 4S isn't just about getting from A to B; it's about the journey in between. With the roof open, you are closer to the elements, hearing the exhaust note echo off the landscape while enjoying the timeless luxury that only a 911 can provide.
