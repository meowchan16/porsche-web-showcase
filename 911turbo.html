The ultimate intersection of supercar speed and luxury refinement.

The 911 Turbo S is the titan of the 911 range. It produces a massive 800 Nm of torque, delivered to all four wheels through an enhanced 8-speed PDK. This allows the car to vanish into the distance with a 0-100 km/h time that rivals the world’s most exclusive hypercars.

But power is nothing without control. Featuring Porsche Active Aerodynamics (PAA) and rear-axle steering, the Turbo S remains incredibly stable at high speeds while being nimble enough for the tightest mountain passes.

Precision in Every Fiber

Inside, the Turbo S offers a leather-clad sanctuary. From the stitching on the dashboard to the carbon fiber trim, every detail is a testament to Porsche's commitment to quality. It is a car that can break track records in the morning and take you across a continent by sunset.
