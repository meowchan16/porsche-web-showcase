The 911 GT3 is the bridge between everyday driving and the racetrack.

Engineered with racing soul, the GT3 features a 4.0-liter naturally aspirated flat-six engine that breathes air without the help of turbos, delivering a raw, mechanical scream that peaks at 9,000 rpm. It is a car built for those who value steering feedback and throttle response over everything else.

With its sophisticated swan-neck rear wing and front diffuser, the GT3 produces immense downforce, keeping it glued to the asphalt through the tightest corners. It’s not just about speed; it's about the precision of every gear shift and the feeling of total control.

The Racing Heart

Whether you choose the lightning-fast PDK or the tactile six-speed manual gearbox, the GT3 remains the definitive sports car. It’s an instrument of speed, designed to transform every road into a racetrack and every drive into an event.
